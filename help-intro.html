How to Use This Application

Logging In

To log in, click on the "Log In" button on the login page and enter your username, name, and email. If your account is found you'll then be taken to the main page with the different fortune features below!

Creating a Profile

Click the "Create Profile" button on the main page and fill in the required fields. Then click "Submit" to create your account. You'll then be taken to the main page with the fortune features!

Using Features

  • Magic 8 Ball: Ask a question, and click "Submit" to get your answer!
  • Fortune Cookie: Click "Crack Open the Cookie" to reveal your fortune!
  • Horoscope: Based on your birthday, view your daily horoscope!
  • Journal: View all your saved fortunes and horoscopes!
